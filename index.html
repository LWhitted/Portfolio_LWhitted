About

Located in Westampton, NJ (open to Hybrid and/or remote work)

Hi, I'm Lauren -- an aspiring Security Operations Anaylst with a background that's taken a few unexpected turns. I hold a bachelor's in Economics and a Master's in Public Health Administration, and I originally set out to climb the HR leadership ladder. For a while, it seemed like that would be my path -- I've built a solid career in HR over the past decade and have truly grown with my current employer.
But tech was always tapping on my shoulder.

Looking back, I probably should've listened sooner. I remember taking a couple of computer science classes in college and loving them, but I didn't think to pursue it further at that time. Fast forward to now -- I've rediscovered that passion through coding, completed a web development certificate via digitalCrafts (Spring 2023), and am nearly finished with the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate. I am also studying for the CompTIA Security+ exam, aiming to take it this fall.

Through shadowing security teams, working closely with InfoSec and privacy leaders, and preparing to support a phishing campaign for one of our sister companies, I'e realized this is where I'm meant to be. Cybersecurity energizes me. It challenges me in all the best ways -- and I can finally say I'm pursing a field I'm truly passionate about.

As a mom of three young girls under 4, I also want to lead by example: to show them that it's never too late to change directions, pursue what you love, and build something new -- even in your 30s.

As a family, we move often because my husband is an active duty Army Major, so I am seeking a hybrid or fully remote role that allows me to bring my growing skill set to a security team that values curiosity, grit, and a strong work ethic.
Thanks for stopping by my portfolio -- I'm excited about where this journey is headed.
